Sifang Xingzhou

Service Design • Floating Pop-up Store for NetEase's "Justice" Game

Project Overview

This cross-disciplinary service innovation design brings the martial arts aesthetics of NetEase's blockbuster game "Justice" from virtual space into physical reality. We designed a modular, floating boat structure serving as an offline brand touchpoint for social interaction.

Users navigate water-based journeys while experiencing immersive interactions and merchandise consumption, exploring new scenarios that merge traditional Chinese culture with modern new retail. The project redefines experiential marketing through spatial storytelling and cultural authenticity.
